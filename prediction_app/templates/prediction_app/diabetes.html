About Diabetes

  • Type 2 diabetes affects how your body processes blood sugar
  • Early detection can prevent serious complications
  • Risk increases with age, obesity, and family history
  • Lifestyle changes can significantly reduce risk

Key Risk Factors

  • High fasting glucose (≥ 126 mg/dL)
  • BMI over 25 (overweight)
  • Family history of diabetes
  • Physical inactivity
  • High blood pressure

Normal Ranges

  • Glucose: 70 – 100 mg/dL
  • Blood Pressure: 60 – 80 mm Hg
  • BMI: 18.5 – 24.9
  • Insulin: 2 – 25 mu U/ml
