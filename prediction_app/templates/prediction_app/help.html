Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the predictions?
Our AI models achieve 81–92% accuracy depending on the disease. Diabetes: 85%, Heart: 87%, Kidney: 83%, Parkinson's: 89%, Breast Cancer: 92%, Liver: 81%. These are trained on real medical datasets.
Are my health records private?
Yes. All your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal health information with third parties. You can delete your account and all data at any time from your profile settings.
Can I use this as a medical diagnosis?
No. MEDIPREDICT is a screening and risk assessment tool, not a medical diagnosis. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
Where do I find my prediction history?
Go to the History page from the navigation bar. You can filter by disease type and date range, and view detailed results for each prediction.
How do I generate a health report?
Go to Reports in the navigation, then click "Generate New Report". You need at least one prediction to generate a report. Reports summarize your risk levels and provide recommendations.
What do the input parameters mean?
Each prediction form has hint text below each field explaining what the parameter means and its normal range. For example, glucose level for diabetes, or cholesterol for heart disease. Ask your doctor if you're unsure about any values.
