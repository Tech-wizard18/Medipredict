About Kidney Disease

Chronic kidney disease (CKD) is a gradual loss of kidney function over time. Early detection is crucial for preventing progression.

Common Symptoms:

  • Fatigue and weakness
  • Swelling in legs, ankles or feet
  • Shortness of breath
  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Changes in urine output
  • Persistent itching
