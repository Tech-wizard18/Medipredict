About Liver Disease

  • The liver performs over 500 vital functions
  • Liver disease is often silent until advanced stages
  • Blood tests can detect liver damage early
  • Lifestyle changes can reverse early liver damage

Warning Signs

  • Yellowing of skin or eyes (jaundice)
  • Abdominal pain and swelling
  • Chronic fatigue
  • Dark urine or pale stool
  • Nausea and vomiting

Normal Lab Ranges

  • Total Bilirubin: 0.1 – 1.2 mg/dL
  • ALT: 7 – 56 IU/L
  • AST: 10 – 40 IU/L
  • Albumin: 3.5 – 5.0 g/dL
  • Total Protein: 6.3 – 8.2 g/dL
