About Breast Cancer

  • Most common cancer in women worldwide
  • Early detection leads to 99% survival rate
  • FNA biopsy is a minimally invasive diagnostic tool
  • Cell nucleus features help classify tumor type

Measurement Guide

  • Mean: average value across all cells
  • SE: standard error of the measurement
  • Worst: largest (most severe) value found
  • Radius: mean distance from center to perimeter

Risk Factors

  • Age over 50
  • Family history of breast cancer
  • BRCA1/BRCA2 gene mutations
  • Dense breast tissue
  • Hormone replacement therapy
