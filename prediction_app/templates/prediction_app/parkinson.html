About Parkinson's

  • A progressive nervous system disorder affecting movement
  • Voice changes are among the earliest detectable signs
  • Affects 1% of people over age 60
  • No cure, but early detection improves management

Voice Analysis

  • Jitter: frequency variation in voice
  • Shimmer: amplitude variation in voice
  • HNR: ratio of harmonic to noise components
  • RPDE/DFA: nonlinear signal complexity measures

Early Symptoms

  • Tremor in hands or fingers
  • Slowed movement (bradykinesia)
  • Rigid muscles
  • Soft or slurred speech
  • Loss of automatic movements
